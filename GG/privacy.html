PRIVACY POLICY

Introduction

At Graphic Greetings, LLC. (graphicgreetings.org), we value the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting their personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, store, use, share, and protect user information, as well as the rights of our users and how they can exercise them.

Data Collection

We collect personal data from our users when they visit our website, create an account, make a purchase, sign up for our newsletter, or interact with our customer support. The information we collect may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Email address
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Telephone number
  • Payment information
  • IP address
  • Browser and device information
  • Usage data, such as pages visited and interaction with our website

Data Usage

We use the collected data to:

  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Provide customer support and communicate with users
  • Personalize user experience and improve our website
  • Send marketing communications, if users have opted-in
  • Monitor website usage and improve security

Data Storage

We store user data on secure servers with appropriate technical and organizational measures in place to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of personal data.

Data Sharing

We may share user data with third-party service providers, such as payment processors, shipping companies, and email marketing providers, for the purposes of fulfilling orders and providing our services. We only share data with third parties who have GDPR-compliant privacy practices in place.

User Rights

Under GDPR, users have the following rights concerning their personal data:

  • Right to access: Users can request a copy of their personal data stored by us.
  • Right to rectification: Users can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to erasure: Users can request that we delete their personal data, subject to certain conditions.
  • Right to restrict processing: Users can request that we limit the processing of their personal data.
  • Right to data portability: Users can request a copy of their personal data in a machine-readable format or request that we transfer their data to another controller.
  • Right to object: Users can object to the processing of their personal data for specific reasons.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Users can withdraw their consent to data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, users can contact us at almoreno@graphicgreetings.org

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or as required by law. We encourage users to review our Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about our data protection practices.
